# Courses

*Estimated individual enrollment prices in **MYR** (as of 2026).*

- 🏢 **Claimable**: Many courses are **HRDF / HRD Corp** claimable.
- 💡 **Cost Savers**: Group discounts and promotions can significantly lower prices.
- 📊 **Pricing Scope**: Short workshops are budget-friendly, while full Python/SQL programs require a larger investment.
*(Note: Listed prices typically exclude 8% SST).*

## Summary & Quick Notes

- **Aidea Big** remains one of the most affordable and practical options for fast **Excel + Power BI** upskilling with a strong self-learn component.
- Prices can drop significantly with **HRD Corp claims**, group/corporate bookings, or current promotions — always check the links for latest offers and intake dates.
- Most providers now emphasize **hybrid learning**: attend live for guidance, then continue with recorded modules at your own pace.
- **Short / Focused courses (1–3 days Excel or Power BI only):** RM1,000 – RM2,500 (best for quick upskilling like Aidea Big Turbo style).
- **Hybrid Data Analytics programs (Python + Power BI + Excel + projects):** RM2,000 – RM4,000 (most common sweet spot for self-paced + live combo).
- **Full certifications/bootcamps:** Can go up to RM5,000 – RM7,000, but many have promos, instalments, or HRD Corp subsidies that reduce your out-of-pocket cost significantly.
- **Factors affecting price:** Live vs self-paced only, inclusion of certification exam, projects/internship support, and group discounts.
