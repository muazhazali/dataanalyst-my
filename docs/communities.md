## Meetup Groups

- **Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Malaysia** (largest AI/ML group, ~1,100+ members)
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-malaysia/](https://www.meetup.com/artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-malaysia/)
  - Facebook: [https://www.facebook.com/groups/aimlmalaysia/](https://www.facebook.com/groups/aimlmalaysia/)
- **Data Council Kuala Lumpur | Data Engineering & Science**
  - Focuses on data engineering, science, platforms, and analytics.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/datacouncil-ai-kuala-lumpur-data-engineering-science/](https://www.meetup.com/datacouncil-ai-kuala-lumpur-data-engineering-science/)
- **PyData Kuala Lumpur**
  - For Python data tools (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, etc.).
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/pydatakl/](https://www.meetup.com/pydatakl/)
- **Malaysia R User Public Group (MyRUG)**
  - Dedicated to R programming for statistics and data analysis.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/malaysia-r-user-group/](https://www.meetup.com/malaysia-r-user-group/)
  - Facebook: [https://www.facebook.com/groups/MalaysiaRUserGroup/](https://www.facebook.com/groups/MalaysiaRUserGroup/) or [https://www.facebook.com/rusergroupmalaysia/](https://www.facebook.com/rusergroupmalaysia/)
- **AWS User Group AI/ML, Kuala Lumpur**
  - AWS-specific AI and machine learning topics.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/awsug-aiml-my/](https://www.meetup.com/awsug-aiml-my/)
- **IBM Cloud - Malaysia**
  - Cloud-focused with strong data, analytics, and AI components.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/ibm-cloud-malaysia/](https://www.meetup.com/ibm-cloud-malaysia/)
- **Microsoft Fabric Community Malaysia**
  - Focuses on Microsoft data and analytics tools (Power BI, Fabric, etc.).
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/kuala-lumpur-microsoft-project-meetup-group/](https://www.meetup.com/kuala-lumpur-microsoft-project-meetup-group/)
- **Elastic Kuala Lumpur User Group**
  - For Elasticsearch, search, and data analytics/visualization.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/kuala-lumpur-elastic-fantastics/](https://www.meetup.com/kuala-lumpur-elastic-fantastics/)
- **ClickHouse Malaysia Meetup Group**
  - Specialized in high-performance analytics database.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/clickhouse-malaysia-meetup-group/](https://www.meetup.com/clickhouse-malaysia-meetup-group/)
- **KL Business Intelligence Pro Group**
  - Business intelligence and analytics professionals.
  - Meetup: [https://www.meetup.com/kl-business-intelligence-pro-group/](https://www.meetup.com/kl-business-intelligence-pro-group/)

You can discover more groups directly on:
- [https://www.meetup.com/topics/data-science/my/](https://www.meetup.com/topics/data-science/my/)
- [https://www.meetup.com/topics/data-analytics/my/](https://www.meetup.com/topics/data-analytics/my/)

## University and Student Clubs

- **Universiti Malaya Data Analytics Club (UMDAC)**
  - Student-led club for data analytics skills, projects, and industry insights.
  - Instagram: [https://www.instagram.com/um_dac/](https://www.instagram.com/um_dac/)
- Other universities (UTM, UTAR, MMU, APU, etc.) often have their own AI/ML or data clubs that host joint events.

## Other Notable Communities and Events

- **AI/ML Malaysia** - events and collaboration platform (see Facebook group above for updates).
- **CDAO Malaysia** - annual conference for Chief Data & Analytics Officers.
  - Official site: [https://cdao-my.coriniumintelligence.com/](https://cdao-my.coriniumintelligence.com/)
- **PyLadies Kuala Lumpur** - often collaborates with PyData KL (women and allies in Python/data).
  - Website: [http://kl.pyladies.com/](http://kl.pyladies.com/)
  - Instagram: [https://www.instagram.com/pyladies_kl/](https://www.instagram.com/pyladies_kl/)
