# Top LinkedIn Creators in Data Science & Machine Learning

Looking to level up your Data Science and ML game? Your LinkedIn feed is one of the best places to start! 

We've curated a list of the most active, insightful creators (as of 2025–2026) who consistently share goldmines of information. From hands-on tutorials and career advice to the latest AI updates, these are the voices you need on your timeline to stay ahead of the curve.

### Must-Follow Creators:

1. **Daliana Liu** – Former Amazon/AWS data scientist; shares ML tips, career growth stories, job search advice, and simplified AI explanations. One of the most recommended DS creators.  
   [linkedin.com/in/dalianaliu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dalianaliu/)

2. **Nick Singh** – Co-author of *Ace the Data Science Interview*, founder of DataLemur; posts SQL challenges, interview prep, career tips, and practical DS content.  
   [linkedin.com/in/nick-singh-tech](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nick-singh-tech/)

3. **Kristin Kehrer (Data Moves Me)** – Data science coach and educator; creates job interview threads, technical tutorials, and guidance for breaking into DS/ML.  
   [linkedin.com/in/kristen-kehrer-datamovesme](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kristen-kehrer-datamovesme/)

4. **Alex Freberg** – Founder of AnalystBuilder; delivers hands-on SQL, Python, data visualization, and analytics tutorials with career strategies.  
   [linkedin.com/in/alex-freberg](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alex-freberg/)

5. **Andrew Ng** – Pioneer in machine learning, founder of DeepLearning.AI; shares AI education, agentic workflows, ML democratization, and industry insights.  
   [linkedin.com/in/andrewyng](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewyng/)

6. **Cassie Kozyrkov** – Former Chief Decision Scientist at Google, CEO of Data Scientific; focuses on decision intelligence, statistics, responsible AI, and practical ML application.  
   [linkedin.com/in/kozyrkov](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kozyrkov/)

7. **Andrej Karpathy** – Founder of Eureka Labs, ex-OpenAI/Tesla; posts deep technical content on LLMs, deep learning, and generative AI.  
   [linkedin.com/in/andrej-karpathy-9a650716](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrej-karpathy-9a650716/)

8. **Andrew Jones** – Founder of Data Science Infinity; creates end-to-end technical tutorials, career advice, and skill-building posts for DS professionals.  
   [linkedin.com/in/andrew-jones-dsi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrew-jones-dsi/)

9. **Megan Lieu** – Rising DS creator; helps beginners with job applications, career challenges, and relatable data science experiences.  
   [linkedin.com/in/meganlieu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/meganlieu/)

10. **Sundas Khalid** – Senior data scientist at Google; shares career transitions, tech journeys, advanced DS tips, and inspiration for women in tech.  
    [linkedin.com/in/sundaskhalid](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sundaskhalid/)
